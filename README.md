# YorkPulse

YorkPulse is a platform built exclusively for York University students. It is accessible only with a yorku.ca email, which keeps the community trusted and campus-specific. The goal is simple: help York students connect, help each other, and exchange resources within the campus community.

---

## Course Chat

Discord servers exist, but they usually only cover the most popular courses and require ongoing management. YorkPulse includes all 7,706 York courses, each with its own chat room. If students want help with assignments, exam prep, or just want to connect with classmates, they can do it there.

When five students add the same professor, a private section chat unlocks automatically so people in the same class can talk directly. After six months, section channels reset to keep professor listings current each semester. The general course channel remains permanently.

---

## Marketplace

Students often buy things for a course and never use them again. For example, a student taking CHEM 1001 as an elective may have to buy a lab coat and materials that become useless after the course ends. Instead of the next student buying everything again at full price from the bookstore, the previous student can sell those items for less. One student recovers some money and the incoming student saves money.

Because the platform is York-specific, scammers and spammers are significantly reduced. Students who abuse the platform can be reported and dealt with accordingly.

---

## Side Quests

Some activities require people, but not everyone is comfortable approaching strangers. If someone is introverted, or their friends are busy, they can post something like "Looking for a soccer group" or "Study session at Scott Library at 6pm." Others who want the same activity can join the post and connect through a group chat.

Side Quests removes the social barrier and loneliness that many students face, making it easier to meet people naturally through shared interests rather than cold introductions.

---

## Quick Gigs

Many students have useful skills but struggle to find small opportunities to use them. Quick Gigs allows students to offer services like tutoring, technical help, moving assistance, design work, or spreadsheet support.

For example, a student moving out might need someone with a license to rent a U-Haul and help for a couple of hours. Instead of paying high market rates, they can connect with another student offering help at a reasonable price. Both sides benefit.

---

## The Vault

The Vault is an anonymous forum where students can post freely without revealing their identity. It makes it easier to ask questions, share opinions, or talk about things that might feel uncomfortable to say publicly. Posts and comments can be made anonymously, keeping the space open and low-pressure.
